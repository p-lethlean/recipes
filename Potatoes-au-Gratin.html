Potatoes au Gratin

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups cream , full fat (Note 1)
  • 2 garlic cloves , minced
  • 30g / 2 tbsp unsalted butter , melted
  • .25 kg / 2.5 lb starchy potatoes , Russet, Sebago, Maris Piper (Note 2)
  • tsp salt
  • /4 tsp pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups gruyere cheese (or mozzarella) , freshly grated yourself (Note 3)
  • 2 tsp thyme leaves , fresh (optional – but highly recommended)

Directions

  1. Cream Mixture: Place butter, cream and garlic in a jug. Mix until combined.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F (both fan and standard ovens).
  3. Slice potatoes: Peel the potatoes and slice them 1/8"/3 mm thick. Or use a slicer!
  4. Layer 1: Spread 1/3 of the potatoes in a baking dish (Note 3), then pour over 1/3 of the Cream Mixture, scatter
  5. with 1/3 of the salt, pepper and thyme. Sprinkle with 3/4 cups cheese.
  6. Layers 2 & 3: Repeat for the 2nd and third layer, but do not finish with cheese on the top layer (will add
  7. later).
  8. Cover & bake: Cover with lid or foil, and bake for 1 hr 15 min or until the potatoes in the middle are soft (use
  9. knife to test).
  10. Top with cheese, bake again: Remove foil, top with cheese. Bake for a further 10 to 15 minutes until golden and
  11. bubbly. Stand 5 minutes before serving.
